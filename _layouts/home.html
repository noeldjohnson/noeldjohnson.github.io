I'm a Professor in the Economics Department at George Mason University.

My research interests include:

  • The historical origins of modern liberal institutions. You can purchase my book (written with Mark Koyama) here: Persecution & Toleration: The Long Road to Religious Freedom. Here's a piece I wrote on why the cover of the book is cool: "Paintings, Persecutions, and Political Development".
  • The relationship between state capacity and economic growth.
  • The impact of environmental shocks on institutional change and economic and social outcomes.
  • Applied Econometrics with a particular emphasis on spatial and text data.
